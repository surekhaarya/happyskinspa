Our Story

A Private
Sanctuary
for Your Skin

Welcome to Happy Skin Spa — a boutique, by-appointment skin care studio nestled in the comfort of a private home setting. Here, every treatment is crafted with intention, care, and your unique skin in mind.

Unlike busy commercial spas, our intimate space allows for unhurried, personalized attention — just you, your skin, and an expert dedicated entirely to your well-being.

We specialize in advanced facials and targeted skin treatments using premium products, calming techniques, and a deep knowledge of skin health. Whether you're looking to address specific concerns or simply indulge in a moment of pure relaxation, you're in the right place.
